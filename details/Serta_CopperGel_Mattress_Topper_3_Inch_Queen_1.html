The Serta CopperGel Mattress Topper

Designed for a night of uninterrupted rest, this 3-inch thick mattress topper combines advanced cooling technology with firm, pressure-relieving support. The copper-infused gel beads actively draw heat away from your body, keeping you cool and comfortable all night long. Whether you're battling restless nights or looking for the perfect way to stay cool in warm weather, this topper delivers the relief you need.

With its high-density memory foam, the topper conforms to your body shape, offering personalized support that promotes better sleep and reduces tossing and turning. This is especially beneficial for those with back, joint, or muscle discomfort, helping to align your spine naturally for a restful experience. The breathable design allows air to circulate, ensuring a fresh and odor-free sleeping environment all year round.

As a CertiPUR-US certified product made in the USA, the Serta CopperGel Mattress Topper is safe for you and the environment. It provides an extra layer of comfort and durability without adding unnecessary bulk or weight. Perfect for use on any existing mattress, this adaptable solution enhances your sleep quality and lifestyle with every night.
