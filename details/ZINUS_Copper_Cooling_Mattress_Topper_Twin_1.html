ZINUS Copper Cooling Mattress Topper

Transform your sleep with the ZINUS Copper Cooling Mattress Topper, crafted to deliver unmatched comfort and temperature control. Perfect for twin mattresses, this innovative topper harnesses the natural cooling properties of copper to help regulate body heat and maintain a cool, refreshing sleep environment throughout the night.

Its advanced airflow technology works to wick away moisture, keeping your bedding dry and promoting better sleep hygiene. The contoured design offers targeted support for pressure points, helping to alleviate discomfort and ensure even weight distribution for a restful night's sleep. With CertiPUR-US certification, you can trust in the safety and durability of the materials used, making this topper a reliable choice for long-term use.

Designed for easy shipping, the topper is compressed into a single box and expands to full size within 72 hours, making setup simple and hassle-free. Whether you're dealing with a hot sleeping environment or want to enhance your mattress comfort, this breathable and supportive topper is an ideal addition to any bedroom, especially during warmer seasons or for those with sensitive skin.
